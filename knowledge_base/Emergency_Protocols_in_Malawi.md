# Emergency Protocols in Malawi

## National Emergency Numbers
- **Police:** 997  
- **Ambulance:** 998  
- **Fire Brigade:** 999  
- **Road Traffic Accidents:** 118  
- **Disaster Management Affairs (DoDMA):** 109  
- **Anti-Corruption Bureau:** 133  
- **Malawi Defence Force (non-classified emergencies):** 01 794 111  

---

# Page 1: Emergency Protocols in Malawi

## Key Concepts
- Emergency services in Malawi operate using standardized national numbers.
- Police respond to crime, violence, and public safety threats.
- Ambulance services provide medical emergency transport and stabilization.
- Fire Brigade handles fire outbreaks, rescue operations, and hazardous conditions.
- Disaster Management Affairs coordinates national disaster response and early warning systems.

## Additional Details
- When calling, always state your exact location, type of emergency, and number of people affected.
- Emergency response prioritizes children, elderly, and critical injuries.
- Citizens are advised to stay calm and avoid obstructing responders.
- During floods, communities should move to higher ground and follow evacuation protocols.
- Cyclone warnings are distributed through radio, SMS, and district authorities.
